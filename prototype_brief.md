# LifeLens AI - Prototype Brief

## Project Title
LifeLens AI

## Tagline
See. Understand. Act.

## Objective
To help people understand important documents by converting complex information into simple explanations and clear next steps using AI.

## Problem Statement
People often receive bills, notices, bank letters, government forms, and college circulars that are difficult to understand. Missing a deadline, payment, or required action can create problems.

## Proposed Solution
LifeLens AI allows the user to upload a photo of a document. Gemini Vision AI reads and understands the image, then explains it in simple language.

## Core Features
1. Upload document image
2. Identify document type
3. Generate simple summary
4. Extract important details
5. Highlight amount or fees
6. Highlight date or deadline
7. Show risk or warning
8. Suggest next action
9. Give a helpful tip
10. Support simple English, Hindi, and Marathi explanation

## AI Component
LifeLens AI uses Gemini Vision AI for:
- Image understanding
- Text reading
- Context understanding
- Summarization
- Action recommendation

## Target Users
- Students
- Parents
- Senior citizens
- Job seekers
- People who struggle with complex documents
- People who prefer simple explanations

## Tech Stack
- Python
- Streamlit
- Gemini Vision AI

## Impact
LifeLens AI helps users understand important documents faster and take the right action on time.

## Future Enhancements
- PDF upload
