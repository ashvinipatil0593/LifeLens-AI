# 👁️ LifeLens AI

## See. Understand. Act.

LifeLens AI is a simple AI application that helps people understand important documents.

Many people find it difficult to read bills, notices, bank letters, or college circulars because the language is complex. LifeLens AI makes these documents easy to understand.

---

# Problem

People often receive important documents but are not sure what they mean.

Sometimes they miss payment dates or important notices because the information is difficult to understand.

---

# Solution

Upload a document image.

LifeLens AI reads the text using OCR and explains it in simple English, Hindi, or Marathi.

It also tells the user what the document is about and what they should do next.

---

# Features

* Upload document images
* Read text using Tesseract OCR
* AI-powered document explanation
* English, Hindi and Marathi support
* Simple summary
* Important details
* Warning or risk (if any)
* Suggested next action
* Helpful tips

---

# Technologies Used

* Python
* Streamlit
* Groq AI
* Tesseract OCR
* Pillow
* python-dotenv

---

# How It Works

1. Upload a document image.
2. OCR extracts the text.
3. AI understands the content.
4. The application shows a simple explanation and important information.
